Project Pride

Project Pride is a non-profit club that aims to help nourish confidence in young adults.
Join us!


Club Bio:

Project pride is a club where the high school South mentors work with selected middle-school students to help build confidence, work on teamwork and social skills. The high school South students go to Grover every other week to carry out various team building activities.



all_inclusive

Good Character

All of the peer advisers from High School South are easy to approach and friendly to work with. Our goal is to help build confidence and make both the high-school student peers and the middle schoolers more outgoing and confident.

group

Teamwork

Teamwork builds character - that is why we focus on having the students work together through various tasks and activities. Some examples include: eating snacks, playing games and activities, and charitable drive donations. Project Pride is a club that brings everyone together!

language

All Inclusive

At Project Pride, everyone is inclusive and outreaching. We focus on having our activities engaging and alluring - so that everyone can participate. There is always place for everyone here, no matter what we are doing.

Project Pride strives to bring everyone together - to have fun!
